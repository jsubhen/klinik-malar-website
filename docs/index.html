About Dr. Kamalamalar Nadarajah

Dr. Kamalamalar Nadarajah is a highly experienced dentist with over 40 years of dedication to the field of dentistry. For more than 30 of those years, she served the Rawang community as a government dental practitioner, primarily at the Rawang government dental clinic. After retiring from public service, she established Klinik Pergigian Malar to continue her passion for providing exceptional dental care. Dr. Kamalamalar is known for her warm, patient-centered approach and has worked with patients of all ages throughout her career.
